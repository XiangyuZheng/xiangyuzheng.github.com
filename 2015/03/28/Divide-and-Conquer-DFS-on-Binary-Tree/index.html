Divide and Conquer & DFS

##Questions
There are many problems about divide and conquer:
Merge sort
Quick sort
Most of B-tree questions.

##Solution
For divide and conquer questions. There are two steps:

  1. Divide. Solve sub-problems.
  2. Conquer. Calculate result value based on child results.

Specially, for B-trees, the two steps becomes:

  1. Divide. Solve problems on left sub-tree and right sub-tree. Sometimes sub-trees are invalid, then usually -1 will be returned.
  2. Conquer. Calculate result value based on sub-results.

##Template
DFS, D&C, Bottom-up
