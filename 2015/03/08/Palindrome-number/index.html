[Leetcode] Palindrome number

##Question
Determine whether an integer is a palindrome. Do this without extra space.

Note that negtive numbers are regarded as non-palindrome.

##Solution

####If you want to convert the integer into a string, it works, but you will use extra space.

####If you want to firstly revert the integer, you need to carefully handler the overflow problem.

####A better way is to comapre the digits one by one from both sides of the integer.
